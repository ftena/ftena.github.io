Tarlic Privacy Policy

1. Sharing data

We never share your data with anyone. We don't transmit or store personal information on our servers.

2. Storing data

Tarlic only stores the data it needs to function properly. This information includes times and date of calls and duration of calls in order to interact with the app. All these information is only stored locally in your device and will be only used by the app.

3. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may at any time with or without a separate notice change this Privacy Policy, and you are encouraged to review this Policy from time to time.

4. Contact

If you have any additional questions about this Privacy Policy or want to make a request regarding certain information, you are encouraged to contact us. You can send your enquiries via email to tarod.net@gmail.com
